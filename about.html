About Me


My personal blog

Hi, my name is Yufan Zou. I graduated from the University of New South Wales in Australia with a bachelor's degree in Computer Science. Currently, I am studying Computer Science at Northeastern University for my master's degree.

In 2020, I started working as a full-time Back-End software engineer at OPPO, which is a top smart device manufacturing company. During this experience, I obtained advanced knowledge of Java, like Java source code, multithreading, and Spring framework. Besides, I participated in building a project from scratch, like system design, database design, RESTful API design, writing unit tests, optimization, and maintenance.

By thoroughly understanding user requirements and overall system architecture, I can deliver products with great performance and an easy-to-use experience. I am also happy to learn new technologies and incorporate them into my work. Learning and curiosity always refresh my mind with better designs and solutions.
